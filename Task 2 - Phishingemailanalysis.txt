Task 2 - Phishing Email Analysis

Email Subject:
EFT Activity Notification (Your payment is processing)

Sender:
Peoples Bank

Phishing Indicators Found:

1. The email creates urgency by asking the user to verify a payment immediately.

2. It instructs the user to log in and check account activity, which is a common phishing technique.

3. The sender claims to be a bank, which is a common form of brand impersonation.

4. The email originally contained links (removed in this example). Phishing emails often use fake links to steal credentials.

5. The email does not address the recipient by name, making it appear like a mass-sent message.

6. The sender's actual email address is not shown, so its authenticity cannot be verified.

Conclusion:

Based on the above indicators, this email appears suspicious and should be treated as a phishing email. Users should avoid clicking any links or providing personal or banking information without verifying the sender.
